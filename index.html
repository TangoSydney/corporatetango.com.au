Corporate Tango develops communication, leadership, teamwork, connection, commitment, confidence, creativity, and group alignment within a unique, innovative and fun dance environment.

The way people dance Argentine Tango is a microcosm of how they are in life. Tango is a journey, not a destination. By increasing people's abilities on the dance floor, they are empowered to overcome self-limiting beliefs and perform better in life, at both professional and personal levels.

No matter how successful and confident a person appears to be at work, the tango floor is a level playing field!

Choose your level of commitment:

  • Social: Employees learn to dance in a fun, group environment as a one-time or ongoing activity (e.g. conference, social event, or weekly).
  • Team: As per Social, with greater emphasis on awareness, trust, respect, communication, and cohesion of relationships.
  • Leadership: Unique program for individual senior leaders (or potential leaders) encompassing dance and mentoring (optional). Fast-tracks learning and provides more in-depth feedback.
  • Performance: Have tango dancers perform at your event. Or, take the ultimate challenge whereby YOU perform at your event!

Corporate Tango is based in Sydney. Travel to other locations is possible.

Principal has a multidisciplianary, international, corporate background and has taught tango since 2001.

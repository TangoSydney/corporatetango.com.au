Social

Social dancing provides the personal interaction which is often missing in today's busy, technology-focused environment.

Corporate Tango's Social program

  • Provides a fun way for people to meet and interact socially
  • Acts as an ice-breaker or fun activity at corporate social events
  • Facilitates integration of staff from the same or different work areas
  • Offers a flexible format to meet your social needs
  • Hourly or weekly

It is suitable for people of all ages and fitness levels - basically, anyone who can walk. All you need are shoes that slide. No previous dance experience is necessary.

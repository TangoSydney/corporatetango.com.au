Performance

Argentine Tango danced well is passionate, sensual, elegant, romantic, intuitive, improvised, heartfelt. It is created "in the moment" from a variety of basic steps and hence is a pure form of communication - without words - a moving meditation to music.

Experience the magic and be emotionally uplifted!

Corporate Tango's Performance program offers 3 options:

  • Performance demonstration by experienced Argentine Tango dancers of 1-3 dances at your corporate event.
  • Performance demonstration by YOUR personnel - under the guidance of Corporate Tango. (Recommended after initial learning program) This is the ultimate embodiment of team alignment, creativity and expression.
  • Combination of the above.

For more information feel free to contact us.
